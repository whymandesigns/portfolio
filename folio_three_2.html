Project Background

In 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic forced Audi to close its showrooms, disrupting the car-buying experience. To adapt, Audi Business Innovation (ABI) set out to develop a digital platform that would recreate the showroom experience, seamlessly integrating online and offline touchpoints for a more cohesive purchasing journey.

I together with a full-stack product team from Modus Create, joined forces with Audi Business Innovation, to bring this vision to life and redefine how customers explore and customize their Audi vehicles.

Objectives

My objectives was on two key feature applications: finalizing the Automotive Visualization app, which enhanced the 3D car customization experience, and overhauling the Audi & Partner Dealer Search, making it easier for customers to find dealerships, explore services, and plan their visit.
