Description

Building a handy and accessible app to motivate people quit their smoking habits.

Context

The Quitter Circle app was a collaborative initiative between Pfizer and the American Lung Association, developed to empower individuals striving to quit smoking. The project was entrusted to me, through Modus Create, to design and build an MVP hybrid app using the Ionic framework.

The app aimed to foster a supportive community by providing resources, tools, and a shared platform where users could connect, share their journey, and encourage one another in their efforts to quit cigarettes. This innovative approach combined technology with community-driven support to address one of the most challenging health goals—smoking cessation.
