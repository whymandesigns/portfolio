Description

Scaling the design practice during Toptal's hypergrowth phase

Context

As Toptal experienced hypergrowth, doubling in size over two years, it became clear that the design practice needed to scale rapidly to keep up with evolving company initiatives. While the design team had established solid system foundations, they were struggling with design consistency across their three main user-facing platforms (Staff, Client, Talent Portals), multiple internal and external tools, and numerous new initiatives.

With so many projects running in parallel, a lack of cohesion led to frequent custom designs that carried over into the implementation phase, increasing costs and debt and reducing the ROI for every product-focused team member. Maintaining consistency became an ever-greater challenge as the company's ecosystem continued to expand.
